Pension Portal

In our pension portal there are three different types of pensions
1.Indira Gandhi National oldage pension scheme
2.Indira Gandhi National widow pension scheme
3.Indira Gandhi National disability pension scheme
Then two options are displayed and the user can select all states or a particular state. If input is all state,then fetching details of people who are elgibile for the given pension is done. If input is multiple states,fetching details of poeple from nopension who are elgibile for the given pension and resides in any of the given state If the input is all areas,fetching details of poeple who are elgibile and resides in the given state. A particular area is selected by entering the pincode and fetching details of people who are elgibile and resides in the given specified area. Final result contains list of individuals who are elgible and yet not covered for the given pension.
